Africa-MIS Project

Overview of the Africa-MIS initiative and mission

Africa-MIS

The Africa-MIS Project is ILS International's flagship initiative — a comprehensive offline digital mapping and ministry information system for Sub-Sahara Africa.

What is Africa-MIS?

Africa-MIS combines topographic maps, country data, people group information, and language data into a single offline-accessible system designed for field workers in Sub-Sahara Africa.

Coverage

Africa-MIS covers all countries of Sub-Sahara Africa with multiple levels of detail:

  • Country-level topographic maps
  • City and town atlases
  • Village-level detail for key countries
  • People group and language overlays

Tools Included

  • Africa-MIS Cities Atlas
  • Africa-MIS Towns Atlas
