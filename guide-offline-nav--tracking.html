Guide: Offline Navigation & Tracking

This guide explains how to use ILS digital maps with the Avenza Maps app for offline GPS navigation and field tracking — no internet or cell service required.

What You Need

  • A smartphone or tablet (iOS or Android)
  • The free Avenza Maps app
  • Downloaded ILS GeoPDF maps for your region

How It Works

GeoPDF maps from ILS are georeferenced — meaning GPS coordinates are embedded in the PDF. Load them into Avenza Maps and your device's GPS will show your real-time position on the map, even without any internet connection.
