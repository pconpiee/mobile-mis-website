Avenza Maps App

The Avenza Maps app is the free companion app for ILS GeoPDF maps. Available for iOS and Android, it allows real-time GPS tracking on offline maps.

Key Features

  • Works completely offline — no internet or cell service needed
  • Real-time GPS location on ILS maps
  • Drop pins, record tracks, measure distances
  • Import ILS GeoPDF map files directly
  • Free to download and use
