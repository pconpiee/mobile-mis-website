Low-Cost Mobile Tools

ILS provides guidance on selecting affordable smartphones and tablets that reliably run ILS eTools and the Avenza Maps app in the field.

Key Considerations

  • Works on: Laptops, PCs, Macs, iPad, iPhone, iPod, Android tablets and phones, Microsoft OS tablets
  • Uses the free Adobe Acrobat Reader — no special software required
  • GPS functionality for navigation (in smartphones and tablets)
  • Low cost, durable, widely available in Africa
